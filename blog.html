Effective Study Tips for Students

Discover essential strategies to enhance your study habits and improve academic performance.

Studying effectively involves finding the right environment, managing time well, and using active learning techniques. Here are some tips:

  • Set specific goals for each study session.
  • Use flashcards for memorization.
  • Take regular breaks to avoid burnout.

ABOUT US

Hilal Public Secondary School is a pioneer in Ghuski, a remote area in Sunsari District. Established in 1996, it now educates over 1,100 students from Nursery to Class 12, about 400 kilometers from Kathmandu, and instituted in a unique location in the Koshi province of Nepal.
