Hilal Public Secondary School

OUR DISTINCTION

  • Balanced syllabus with religious and conventional subjects including Tahfeezul Quran Facility
  • Separate classes for Boys & Girls
  • Only School of Muslims in this area recognized by Ministry of Education, Gov of Nepal for Secondary Level
  • Uses of Modern Educational Tools & Techniques
  • Well equipped Science & Computer Laboratory
  • Well managed Library with Reading Room
  • Scholarship facilities for meritorious and needy students
  • Free education for orphan students
  • Vocational Education e.g., Sewing & Computer Education
  • Focusing on extra-curricular activities with built-in curriculum to develop students' interpersonal skills and enable them to lead their community
  • Hostel Facilities for Boys
  • AICU Classes for Weak Students


AIM AND OBJECTIVE

  • To educate the children of the Muslim Ummah in the light of Quran & Sunnah
  • To prepare self-made youth with a strong moderate education system
  • To make them aware of the problems and challenges of the community
  • To enable and strengthen them to solve the problems of Muslim Ummah
  • To establish a well-educated and literate Muslim society capable of addressing global challenges and leading the community and nation

ABOUT US

Hilal Public Secondary School is a pioneer in Ghuski, a remote area in Sunsari District. Established in 1996, it now educates over 1,100 students from Nursery to Class 12, about 400 kilometers from Kathmandu, and instituted in a unique location in the Koshi province of Nepal.
