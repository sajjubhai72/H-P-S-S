ABOUT US

Hilal Public Secondary School is a pioneer in Ghuski, a remote area in Sunsari District. Established in 1996, it now educates over 1,100 students from Nursery to Class 12, about 400 kilometers from Kathmandu, and instituted in a unique location in the Koshi province of Nepal.
